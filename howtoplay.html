How To Play

Use the left and right arrow keys to move from lane to lane.

There are three lanes and you have to avoid obstacles by switching between the lanes whilst collecting rubbish to reduce emissions or coins to fund research.

Lane Runner
Jetpack

Use the space bar to use your jetpack.

You are traversing the game with a jetpack. You need to avoid hazardous obstacles whilst collecting coins to fund scientific research and litter to preserve sealife! This research will help develop technology to save the environment!
